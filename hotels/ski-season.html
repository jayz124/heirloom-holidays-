Ski Season

These resorts are peak perfection

Home / Hotel Collections / Ski Season

From the legendary slopes of the French Alps to the powder-perfect runs of the Rockies, the world's finest ski resorts offer far more than just exceptional skiing. These mountain sanctuaries combine thrilling days on the piste with exceptional après-ski, world-class dining, and restorative spas—creating the complete winter experience.

Our Alpine Collection

Peak luxury at the world's finest ski destinations

Courchevel 1850, France

Cheval Blanc Courchevel

Courchevel 1850 is a skier's dream, and Cheval Blanc makes it paradise. The Russian banya and infinity-edge indoor pool are perfect for unwinding, while the three-Michelin-starred Le 1947 restaurant deserves a place on every culinary bucket list. Dog sledding, igloo experiences, and snowmobile races await the adventurous.

Ski-In Ski-Out 3 Michelin Stars Russian Banya

St. Moritz, Switzerland

Badrutt's Palace Hotel

The fairy-tale Badrutt's Palace offers far more than skiing. Dining alone is worth the journey—Jason Atherton's King's Social House and Nobu Matsuhisa's Japanese-Peruvian creations await. Between runs, try the hotel's private ice rink, curling, scenic toboggan rides, or snowshoe treks through the enchanting Engadin valley.

Historic Icon Nobu Restaurant Private Ice Rink
Begin Your Journey
Andermatt, Switzerland

The Chedi Andermatt

GHM's first European property brings Asian serenity to the Swiss Alps. Jean-Michel Gathy's design features glossy alpine wood, over 200 fireplaces, and a 115-foot pool against stunning mountain views. The comprehensive spa offers Thai reflexology while fine dining at The Japanese restaurant elevates the alpine experience.

35m Indoor Pool Asian-Inspired Spa Cigar Lounge
Begin Your Journey
Courchevel 1850, France

L'Apogée Courchevel

A classic chalet with official French Palace status—complete with its own aerodrome. Ski-in, ski-out access to all 600 kilometres of Les Trois Vallées, a La Prairie spa for post-piste pampering, and a sushi restaurant make this intimate retreat feel like a gilded gingerbread house in the clouds.

Palace Status La Prairie Spa Own Aerodrome
Begin Your Journey
Whistler, Canada

Four Seasons Resort Whistler

You don't need skis to enjoy this winter wonderland—though Whistler's legendary terrain awaits. The spa's unique Canadian maple syrup and brown sugar scrub rejuvenates après-ski, while the outdoor heated pool steams against falling snow. Dog sledding, bobsleighing, and snowshoeing round out the adventure.

Outdoor Pool Dog Sledding Village Shuttle
Begin Your Journey
Aspen, Colorado

The Little Nell

Aspen is famous for its slopes, but the après-ski scene is where it truly shines. The Living Room bar buzzes with hot toddies and champagne around the fireplace. Ajax Tavern serves legendary Wagyu burgers for people-watching, while Element 47's gourmet offerings pair with a world-class wine cellar.

Ski-In Ski-Out Wine Cellar Legendary Après
