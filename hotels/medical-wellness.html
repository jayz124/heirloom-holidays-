Weggis, Switzerland

Chenot Palace Weggis

Dubbed the world's best detox programme for three years running, Chenot Palace overlooks Lake Lucerne with the Swiss Alps as backdrop. Dr. Henri Chenot's pioneering method integrates cutting-edge technology with traditional practices—from advanced diagnostics to 850-calorie gourmet cuisine that tastes anything but restrictive.

SHA Wellness Clinic

A sleek, white-washed paradise overlooking the Mediterranean, SHA combines macrobiotic cuisine with cutting-edge treatments. Clinique La Prairie

A byword for luxury and wellness since 1931, this legendary Swiss institution pioneered longevity medicine. The Master Detox programme uses cutting-edge technology and personalised nutrition to cleanse from the inside out. With Lake Geneva and the Alps as backdrop, every moment here feels like a step toward lasting vitality.
