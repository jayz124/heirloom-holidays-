Best for Families

For you and your little emperors

The secret to parental bliss is simple: exceptional kids' clubs. These hotels have cracked the code, offering little ones a world of adventure while grown-ups enjoy uninterrupted relaxation. From tropical retreats with ziplines to English country estates with Shetland ponies, these properties keep the whole family happy.

Our Family Collection

Where grown-ups and little ones both thrive

Maldives

Soneva Jani

The Den is an epic two-story playground—one of Asia's largest kids' clubs. Young explorers can zipline through cascading waterfalls, splash in pools, explore a pirate ship, or catch air on a skateboard ramp. This technology-free experience turns children's dreams into reality.

The Den Water Slides Teen Zone

Hampshire, England

Four Seasons Hampshire

Little ones have a blast with complimentary arts, crafts, and games while parents unwind. Sharkie's Reef pool features water slides, and young equestrians can explore the countryside with horseback rides—or simply bond with resident Shetland ponies, Milo and Dougal.

Kids' Club Shetland Ponies Water Slides
Plan Your Family Escape
Phuket, Thailand

Rosewood Phuket

The Rosewood Explorers Club features climbing walls, arts and crafts, a performance stage, and cosy reading nooks. Beyond the fun, children discover sustainability with eco walks and recycled paper making. Ocean adventures, plant-based cooking, and Thai culture round out the experience.

Explorers Club Climbing Wall Eco Learning
Plan Your Family Escape
Tenerife, Spain

The Ritz-Carlton Tenerife, Abama

Stargazing, water sports, and adventures for all ages await. The Ritz Kids garden, outdoor playground, and Planetarium room make boredom impossible. Even tiny adventurers aged 0-3 are welcome, and family dining at Michelin-starred restaurants M.B and Kabuki is the icing on the cake.

Planetarium Stargazing 2 Michelin Stars

Mauritius

JW Marriott Mauritius Resort

The Kite Flyers club offers energetic, creative activities for ages 4-12. Teens get their own club with the latest technology, beach parties, and late-night movies. Parents can enjoy kite-surfing with top-notch equipment and personalised tuition while the little ones play.

Kids 4-12 Teen Club Watersports

St Tropez, France

Airelles Saint-Tropez, Château de la Messardière

The Summer Camp features imaginative pirate and mermaid-themed décor, a swimming pool, arcade games, and a private cinema. Pure fun for children aged 4 and over, while parents enjoy the French Riviera sunshine knowing their little ones are having the time of their lives.

Private Cinema Arcade Games Themed Play
