Mahé, Seychelles

Cheval Blanc Seychelles

Private villas cascade along beach and hillside on Mahé's southwest coast, each with its own infinity pool. Five restaurants, a Guerlain spa, and a setting designed for complete privacy and relaxation mark Cheval Blanc's spectacular Seychelles debut.

Venice, Italy

Airelles Venezia

Airelles' first venture outside France transforms three historic buildings on the island of Giudecca into a Venetian masterpiece. Three pools, three restaurants, and even a private church—prepare for their legendary culinary team to redefine la dolce vita.

Island Location 3 Pools Private Church

New York, USA

Faena New York

Landing in West Chelsea with 120 rooms, a South American-inspired wellness sanctuary, and the nightlife magic that made Faena Miami legendary. The Big Apple just got a little more fabulous—and we're thrilled to have front-row access.

Montana, USA

One&Only Moonlight Basin

One&Only's first American property is an all-season mountain resort with skiing, hiking, and a whiskey shack tucked into the woods. With Yellowstone National Park nearby, it's about connecting to the great outdoors in true One&Only style.

Rosewood Miyakojima

Beachfront villas on a picturesque Japanese island reveal a different side of Japan. Spa treatments, local arts experiences, and a sea turtle conservation programme combine luxury with meaningful connection to place and nature.
