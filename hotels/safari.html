Safari

Take a walk on the wild side

Africa's finest safari lodges offer far more than wildlife encounters—they deliver transformative experiences where luxury meets the untamed wilderness. From the Serengeti's endless plains to Botswana's pristine waterways, these properties combine world-class guiding, extraordinary cuisine, and intimate accommodations where the African bush becomes your private theatre.

Our Safari Collection

Extraordinary encounters in Africa's finest wilderness

Sabi Sand, South Africa

Singita Boulders Lodge

Set along the Sand River's rocky banks, Singita Boulders Lodge is a bold retreat inspired by its rugged surroundings. Floor-to-ceiling glass walls in the 12 suites bring the outdoors in, with expansive decks and heated pools. Twice-daily game drives reveal abundant wildlife, including legendary leopard sightings.

Private Concession Leopard Territory Heated Pools

Cape Town, South Africa

Mount Nelson, A Belmond Hotel

The iconic "Pink Lady" offers the perfect gateway to your African adventure. Set within nine acres of lush gardens at the foot of Table Mountain, this legendary hotel combines Cape Town sophistication with proximity to the Cape Winelands and Garden Route safaris. The ideal start or end to any South African journey.

Safari Gateway Librisa Spa Historic Icon

Dubai, UAE

The Lana, Dorchester Collection

For those combining African safari with Middle Eastern luxury, The Lana offers an exceptional stopover. This architectural masterpiece on Dubai Creek provides the perfect pause in your journey, with world-class dining, an indulgent spa, and the timeless elegance that defines Dorchester Collection properties.

Rosewood Baha Mar

Complete your African odyssey with Caribbean tranquility. After the excitement of safari, Rosewood Baha Mar offers the perfect beach retreat. White sand, turquoise waters, and world-class spa treatments provide the ultimate wind-down from your wilderness adventure.
