Disconnect & Reset

Time off, done properly

Home / Hotel Collections / Disconnect & Reset

In our hyper-connected world, true rest has become the ultimate luxury. These sanctuaries offer more than a break—they provide the space and serenity to reconnect with yourself. From remote island retreats to mountain hideaways, each property has been chosen for its commitment to wellness, tranquility, and the art of slowing down.

Our Wellness Collection

Curated retreats where luxury meets tranquility

Nassau, Bahamas

Rosewood Baha Mar

A Caribbean sanctuary where white sand beaches meet world-class wellness. The Sense Spa offers transformative treatments inspired by local healing traditions, while private cabanas provide ultimate seclusion. Wake to turquoise waters and end your days with sunset yoga on pristine shores.

Sense Spa Private Beach Ocean Views

Paris, France

Mandarin Oriental Lutetia, Paris

This Left Bank icon offers an urban escape where Art Deco elegance meets Asian-inspired wellness. The Akasha Spa is a 700-square-metre sanctuary featuring a stunning swimming pool, holistic treatments, and serene relaxation spaces—a world away from the city's bustle.

Akasha Spa Indoor Pool Urban Sanctuary
Begin Your Journey
Gstaad, Switzerland

Gstaad Palace

A legendary Swiss retreat where Alpine tranquility meets timeless grandeur. The Palace Spa offers comprehensive wellness programs including cryotherapy, Alpine-inspired treatments, and meditation sessions with breathtaking valley views. The ultimate mountain escape for mind, body, and soul.

Palace Spa Mountain Views Wellness Programs
Begin Your Journey
Cape Town, South Africa

Mount Nelson, A Belmond Hotel

Set within nine acres of lush gardens at the foot of Table Mountain, this pink-hued sanctuary offers an oasis of calm. The Librisa Spa blends African healing wisdom with contemporary techniques, while the tranquil gardens provide space for quiet contemplation beneath the African sun.

Librisa Spa Garden Retreat Table Mountain

Courchevel, France

L'Apogée Courchevel

Perched above the clouds in the French Alps, this intimate retreat combines mountain luxury with exceptional spa facilities. The panoramic Alpine views and pristine mountain air create the perfect environment for rejuvenation—whether you're hitting the slopes or simply soaking in the serenity.

Mountain Spa Alpine Views Ski-In Access
