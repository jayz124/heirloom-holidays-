Indonesia

Spiritual Awakening in Bali & Beyond

Where ancient temples rise through emerald rice terraces and every sunset feels like a blessing

Home / Destinations / Bali & Beyond
Back to Destinations

Bali casts a spell that's impossible to resist. This Island of the Gods—where Hindu temples nestle in volcanic landscapes, where rice terraces cascade like giant staircases to the sky, and where spirituality infuses every aspect of daily life—offers a journey that nourishes body, mind, and soul. Beyond Bali, the Indonesian archipelago holds countless treasures waiting to be discovered.

Ubud & the Cultural Heart

Ubud has long been Bali's cultural and spiritual centre, a place where traditional arts flourish and wellness practices run deep. Here, master painters and woodcarvers continue traditions passed down through generations, while yoga studios and healing centres attract seekers from around the world. The Tegallalang rice terraces, just north of town, offer the iconic Balinese landscape—emerald green paddies carved into steep hillsides, tended by farmers using methods unchanged for centuries.

Rise before dawn to climb Mount Batur and watch the sunrise over the caldera. Participate in a traditional purification ceremony at Tirta Empul temple. Take a private cooking class in a local family compound. In Ubud, every experience connects you more deeply to this remarkable culture.

Beach & Beyond

Beyond Ubud's terraced highlands, Bali's coastline offers its own magic. The Bukit Peninsula's dramatic clifftop temples, like Uluwatu perched above the Indian Ocean, provide some of Indonesia's most spectacular settings. Further north, the black volcanic beaches of Lovina offer a quieter alternative, where dolphins play at dawn and traditional fishing boats dot the shore.

The Outer Islands

For those seeking adventure beyond Bali, Indonesia's archipelago holds remarkable treasures. The Komodo Islands offer encounters with the world's largest lizard in landscapes of volcanic drama. Raja Ampat, in West Papua, provides what many consider the planet's finest diving—coral gardens of extraordinary biodiversity in waters so clear they seem to glow. The ancient temples of Java and the traditional villages of Flores offer cultural experiences to rival anything in Asia.

Where to Stay

Extraordinary resorts that honour Balinese traditions while offering world-class luxury and wellness

Amandari, Ubud

Perched above the Ayung River gorge, Amandari was one of the resorts that defined Bali's luxury scene. Designed to resemble a traditional Balinese village, the pavilion-style suites offer sublime privacy while the dramatic infinity pool—seemingly floating above the river valley—remains one of the world's most photographed. The spa draws on ancient Balinese healing traditions.

Valley Views Aman Spa Village Design

Four Seasons Resort Bali at Sayan

Reached by a dramatic bridge spanning the Ayung River, Sayan feels like entering another world. The lotus pond-topped main building seems to float above the jungle canopy, while river-edge suites offer complete immersion in nature. The Sacred River Spa, Dharma Shanti yoga pavilion, and exceptional dining make this a destination for deep wellness.

River Setting Sacred River Spa Yoga Pavilion

Bulgari Resort Bali

Perched on the clifftops above Uluwatu, Bulgari brings Italian glamour to one of Bali's most dramatic locations. The design seamlessly blends traditional Balinese architecture with contemporary Italian style, while the cliff-side location offers breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean. The private beach, reached by inclinator, is one of Bali's most exclusive.

Clifftop Setting Private Beach Italian Design

Nihi Sumba Island

On the remote island of Sumba, Nihi offers what many consider the world's most extraordinary resort experience. The dramatically isolated setting, world-class surf break, and immersive cultural experiences with local Sumbanese communities create something truly unique. This is adventure luxury at its finest—raw, authentic, and utterly unforgettable.

World's Best Resort Private Surf Break Safari Experiences
